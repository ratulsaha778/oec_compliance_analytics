The Compliance Analytics Program is a global initiative to implement an analytics-based approach to strategically identify, review and mitigate Compliance risk and support the elements of a comprehensive Compliance program.

The Compliance Analytics Dashboard is a data-driven, analytics-enabled platform which allows the ability to:
  • Focus and prioritize risk on a local, regional and global level
  • Quickly and more easily measure compliance with guidance and principles
  • Measure the impact of Compliance initiatives, such as new policies and training
  • Achieve broader coverage by considering 100% of available data in selected risk areas across employees, healthcare professionals and third parties
  • Incorporate data sources not typically associated with transactional data to better inform local, regional and global risk profile and business decisions
